Other Resources

Get the Help You Need

Harris Health System
This organization provides dental care and a variety of preventive and primary healthcare services to homeless individuals through 10 shelter-based clinics and through mobile health and dental units.




Coalition For the Homeless
Coalition for the Homeless The Coalition for the Homeless coordinates community projects to prevent and end homelessness and raises money to fund housing projects.




Search
Search educates, employs, and houses homeless individuals and families. Their mobile outreach program offers on-the-spot assessment and treatment from a van carrying food, water, and other critical supplies.




Human Rights Campaign
The Human Rights Campaign in Houston organizes events to raise awareness and support for LGBT and other minority support. They will absolutely provide support and education resources for yourself and family members.




Houston Caucus LGBT
The oldest standing civil rights center in the South, the Houston GLBT Political Caucus is constantly fighting for legal equality for LGBT people in politics and in the community. They are an excellent resource for legal information.




Pride Houston
This organization raises awareness in LGBT issues including health, safety, and marriage equality. They strive to make Houston a more welcoming place for people in the community
